A **BMI calculator (Body Mass Index calculator)** is a simple tool used to check whether a person has a healthy body weight based on their height and weight. It helps identify if someone is underweight, normal weight, or overweight. BMI is calculated using the formula: weight divided by height squared. It gives a quick estimate of body health and is widely used in fitness and medical fields. This tool is useful for understanding personal fitness and making better lifestyle and diet decisions.
